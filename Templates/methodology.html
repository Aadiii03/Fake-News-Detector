Methodology

Technical overview of the hybrid classification pipeline used in Fake News Detector.

1. Data preprocessing

  • Lower-casing, punctuation removal, stop-word filtering.
  • Tokenization and TF-IDF vectorization to represent text numerically.

2. Hybrid model

  • Trained a Naive Bayes (NB) classifier and a Logistic Regression (LR) classifier.
  • Final probability is the mean of NB and LR outputs, improving stability.

3. Evaluation

On the held-out test set the ensemble achieved approximately 96% accuracy with balanced precision and recall for both classes.

4. Limitations

Performance may degrade on new types of misinformation or very recent events. Therefore, users are encouraged to cross-check results on trusted platforms.
