About the Project

Fake News Detector is a hybrid machine-learning project built as an academic prototype to explore automated misinformation detection.

Project goals

  • Assist users in quickly estimating whether a piece of news is likely fake or real.
  • Demonstrate hybrid classification (Naive Bayes + Logistic Regression) on real datasets.
  • Encourage responsible sharing by linking to external fact-checking platforms.

Owner & Team

This prototype has been designed and implemented by Adil Sheikh as a college major project under the team name Impact Makers.

The system is intended strictly for educational and research purposes, not as a replacement for professional journalism.
